Before anything else, I chose the name **PEACH**.

Not for marketing. Not for memorability (well, maybe a litte...).  
But as a **mental scaffold** and operational compass.

## PEACH As Acronyms

**PEACH** stands for multiple acronyms, depending on the context. The payoff is:  
 **P**rocrastination **E**nds **A**nd **C**larity **H**appens.  
Each letter also encodes a principle that guides building, thinking, and action, while directly targeting procrastination:

- **P - Progress over perfection**  
  Every action counts. Perfection is the enemy of moving forward.

- **E - Environment shapes action**  
  Remove friction. Design cues. Nudge behavior. Clarity of context drives execution.

- **A - Accountability can be internal and external**  
  Own decisions and results, and leverage scaffolding from others when needed. Stops avoidance loops.

- **C - Clarity first, complexity later**  
  Decide before designing. Kill uncertainty before scaling effort. Direct antidote to overthinking.

- **H - Habits as leverage**  
  Small, repeatable patterns compound into motion. Reinforces consistency and breaks inertia.

These principles are **constraints**, not slogans. They force decisions and movement instead of procrastination.

## Why Naming Matters

A name is not about logos or domains.  
It's about **how you reason about the project**.

With **PEACH**, every decision can be checked against the acronym:

- Does it increase **Progress**?  
- Does it leverage **Environment**?  
- Does it enforce **Accountability**?  
- Does it bring **Clarity**?  
- Does it build **Habits**?  

If the answer is no, it doesn't pass the filter.

## Optionality vs Alignment

**PEACH** allows experimentation.  
It gives enough openness to pivot, enough constraints to stay coherent.

This is not about perfect definitions.  
It's about *alignment of action, language, and clarity*.

## What This Changes

From this point forward:

- **PEACH** is the container for ideas, experiments, and failures  
- All early artifacts are judged by the acronym  
- Any name, site, or product variation must stay *consistent* with the acronym's meaning  

**PEACH** is now a mental and operational compass that directly fights procrastination and overthinking.  
Everything else flows from it.
